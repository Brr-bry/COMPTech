TYPES OF MOTHERBOARDS

DIFFERENT TYPES OF MOTHERBOARDS AND THEIR KEY FEATURES

  • AT (Advanced Technology) - Older, limited expansion, large connectors
  • ATX - Standard, multiple expansion slots
  • BTX (Balanced Technology Extended) - Better airflow, thermal management
  • E-ATX (Extended-ATX) - Larger, more expansion slots
  • LPX (Low Profile Extended) - Low-profile, integrated components
  • Micro-ATX - Compact, fewer expansion slots
  • Mini-iTX (Information Technology Extended) - Small, low-power, limited expansion
  • Mini-ATX - Smaller than ATX, moderate expansion
  • Pico BTX - Ultra-compact, thermal optimization
  • Standard-ATX - Most common, full-featured, expandable
