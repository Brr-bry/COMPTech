TYYPES OF COMPUTERS

DIFFERENT TYPES OF COMPUTERS AND THEIR KEY FEATURES

  • Supercomputer - High performance, massive processing power, used for complex simulations and calculations.
  • Mainframe Computers - Large-scale data processing, reliable, used for business and government applications.
  • Mini Computers - Mid-range processing power, smaller than mainframes, used in small to medium-sized businesses.
  • Server - Provides services or resources to other computers over a network, high storage capacity, and reliable performance.
  • Workstations - High-performance personal computers, used for tasks like graphic design, engineering, and scientific applications.
  • Micro Computers - Small, personal computers, commonly used in homes and offices, designed for general-purpose use.
  • Mini-iTX (Information Technology Extended) - Small, low-power, limited expansion
